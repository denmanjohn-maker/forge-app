# RESEARCH_COMPETITIVE_EVAL_2026-06-02

## Objective
Evaluate leading MTG deck-management platforms and identify high-impact features forge-app can add to strengthen product-market fit and differentiation.

## Competitive Snapshot

### Moxfield
- Best-in-class deck builder UX
- Strong social loop (likes, comments, follows, activity feed)
- Deck version history and reusable card packages
- Integrations (EDHREC recommendations, combo tooling)

### Archidekt
- Visual drag-and-drop builder and strong playtesting flow
- Deck diff/comparison workflow
- In-editor combo visibility and tagging depth
- Community roadmap/feature voting

### EDHREC
- Commander recommendation engine (synergy/nonbo/salt signals)
- Powerful discovery workflows by commander/theme/budget
- Strong data moat from broad decklist aggregation

### MTGGoldfish
- Metagame/tournament intelligence leader
- Strong pricing and finance visibility
- Competitive deck archetype tracking

### Deckbox / ManaBox (Collection-focused)
- Collection and trade workflows are strongest in class
- Ownership-aware deck decisions are a key retention vector
- ManaBox demonstrates strong mobile/offline value

## Key Gaps in Current Market
1. No major platform combines top-tier deck building + collection intelligence + metagame analytics + modern AI workflow in one product.
2. AI support is fragmented and mostly absent in incumbent products.
3. Mobile experience remains weak among top web incumbents.
4. Collection-to-deck and “what can I build now?” workflows are underdeveloped.

## Highest-Value Features for forge-app

### Priority 1 (Immediate)
1. **Deck Diff / Compare**
   - Compare two versions or two decks with clear card-level deltas.
2. **Reusable Card Packages**
   - Save/insert package templates (mana base, ramp suite, interaction core).
3. **Deck Primer + Explainability**
   - Structured strategy notes plus AI-generated deck rationale.
4. **Advanced Deck Analytics**
   - Curve, color distribution, role balance, and consistency indicators.

### Priority 2 (Near-term)
1. **Collection-Aware Building**
   - Owned/missing overlays, acquisition list, and “build from my collection”.
2. **Win-Rate & Matchup Tracking**
   - Lightweight game logging with matchup trends and archetype performance.
3. **Community Discovery Layer**
   - Better deck search, tags, filtering, and social ranking signals.

### Priority 3 (Differentiation)
1. **AI Brewing Copilot**
   - Interactive build iteration, substitutions, and matchup tuning.
2. **Meta-Aware Suggestions**
   - Recommendations that blend user goals with evolving format metagames.
3. **Mobile-First/PWA Experience**
   - Fast browse, deck edits, and playtest-friendly flows on phone.

## Suggested Positioning for forge-app
“AI-native MTG deck platform: build faster, tune smarter, and manage your real collection in one workflow.”

## Success Metrics
- Feature adoption rate (diff, packages, AI explanation, collection-aware suggestions)
- Deck creation-to-publish conversion
- Weekly returning deck builders
- Win-rate logging participation and retention
- Average session depth across build + analysis + iteration

## Recommendation
Prioritize features that bridge **deck building + explainable AI + collection context** before adding heavy social complexity. This creates a clear wedge against incumbents and reinforces forge-app’s strongest differentiator.
